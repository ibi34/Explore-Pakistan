About Skardu Valley

Skardu is the central valley of the Gilgit-Baltistan region. Surrounded by the towering peaks of the Karakoram range, it is known for its breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude deserts, and crystal-clear lakes.

Whether you want to witness the magic of Shangrila Resort or cross the cold desert of Katpana, Skardu is the ultimate destination for adventure and peace.

  • ✔ Shangrila Resort & Lower Kachura Lake
  • ✔ Cold Desert (Katpana)
  • ✔ Deosai National Park
  • ✔ Shigar Fort & Valley

Top Places to Visit in Skardu

Deosai Plains

The second-highest alpine plateau in the world, famous for its lush green meadows and wild bears.

Satpara Lake

A stunning natural lake that supplies water to Skardu Valley, perfect for trout fishing and boating.

Katpana Desert

The world's highest cold desert where you can enjoy sand dunes surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Things To Do in Skardu

Boating in Kachura

Enjoy peaceful boating in the crystal-clear waters of Upper and Lower Kachura Lakes.

Cold Desert Safari

Experience an adventurous jeep safari and dirt biking in the world's highest cold desert, Katpana.

Fort Exploration

Hike up to the ancient Kharpocho Fort for a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire Skardu city.

Deosai Wildlife

Spot the rare Himalayan Brown Bear and beautiful wildflowers while camping in Deosai National Park.
