Murree & Galiyat

Relax in the cool weather, thick pine forests, and scenic hill views of Pakistan's most loved hill station.

About Murree

Murree is the most popular hill station in Pakistan, located just an hour's drive from Islamabad. With its colonial-era buildings, vibrant Mall Road, and towering Chinar trees, it offers a quick and beautiful escape from city life.

Adjacent to Murree is the stunning Galiyat region, including Nathia Gali and Ayubia, known for their peaceful hiking tracks, wildlife, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Top Places to Visit in Murree

Mall Road

The bustling heart of Murree, filled with traditional food stalls, handicraft shops, and cafes.

Patriata (New Murree)

Famous for its world-class chairlift and cable car system offering bird's-eye views of the forests.

Nathia Gali

A picturesque hill resort known for its peaceful environment, foggy weather, and hiking trails.

Things To Do in Murree

Chairlift Rides

Enjoy scenic chairlift and cable car rides in Patriata and Ayubia.

Pipeline Track Hiking

Walk the beautiful Dunga Gali to Ayubia Pipeline Track in the woods.

Winter Snowfall

Visit in winter to experience the magical snowfall and make snowmen.

Shopping & Food

Enjoy hot coffee, Kashmiri tea, and shop for local handicrafts on Mall Road.
