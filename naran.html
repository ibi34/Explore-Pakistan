Naran Kaghan

Discover lush green meadows, fast-flowing rivers, and cool weather in the heart of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

About Naran Kaghan

Naran and Kaghan are two stunning alpine valleys that attract thousands of tourists every summer. Known for the magical Lake Saif-ul-Malook and the mighty Kunhar River, it is the perfect escape from the scorching summer heat.

From the heights of Babusar Top to the peaceful vibes of Shogran, this valley offers something for everyone—be it a family vacation or an extreme adventure.

  • ✔ Lake Saif-ul-Malook
  • ✔ Babusar Pass (13,700 ft)
  • ✔ Shogran & Siri Paye
  • ✔ River Rafting in Kunhar

Top Places to Visit in Naran

Lake Saif-ul-Malook

A mesmerizing alpine lake surrounded by snow-clad mountains and steeped in legendary folklore.

Babusar Top

The highest point in the Kaghan Valley offering stunning panoramic views connecting KP to Gilgit.

Lulusar Lake

The primary headwaters of the Kunhar River, known for its mirror-like reflections and serene beauty.

Things To Do in Naran

River Rafting

Experience extreme thrill with white-water rafting in Kunhar River.

Jeep Safari

Take an adventurous 4x4 jeep ride to Lake Saif-ul-Malook or Siri Paye.

Trout Fishing

Catch fresh Brown and Rainbow Trout from the icy waters of Kunhar.

Camping

Setup camps at Batakundi or Lulusar for a peaceful night under the stars.
