About Swat Valley

Known as the "Switzerland of the East," Swat Valley is a place of fairy-tale beauty. With its roaring rivers, dense pine forests, and alpine meadows, it is a top destination for both nature lovers and history enthusiasts.

From the thrilling ski slopes of Malam Jabba to the serene waters of Mahodand Lake in Kalam, Swat offers an unforgettable experience wrapped in Pashtun hospitality.

Top Places to Visit in Swat

Malam Jabba

Pakistan's most famous ski resort, offering thrilling chairlift rides, ziplining, and winter sports.

Kalam Valley

A mesmerizing valley surrounded by lush green hills, thick forests, and the beautiful Swat River.

Mahodand Lake

A breathtaking glacial lake in Upper Ushu Valley, perfect for boating and trout fishing.

Things To Do in Swat

Skiing & Chairlift

Enjoy winter sports and scenic chairlift rides at Malam Jabba Resort.

River Rafting

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting in the cold Swat River.

Trout Fishing

Catch and enjoy delicious fresh trout near Mahodand Lake.

Explore Heritage

Visit ancient Buddhist Stupas and the Swat Museum in Mingora.
