Fairy Meadows

Trek into the heart of the mountains and camp under the shadow of the mighty Nanga Parbat.

About Fairy Meadows

Fairy Meadows, locally known as Joot, is a lush green plateau located at the base of Nanga Parbat, the 9th highest mountain in the world. It is famously known as the "Killer Mountain," but the meadows themselves offer unparalleled peace and beauty.

The journey is an adventure in itself, starting with a thrilling jeep ride from Raikot Bridge, followed by a scenic hike through alpine forests to reach the spectacular campsite.

Top Places to Explore

Fairy Meadows Campsite

The main grassy meadow surrounded by thick pine forests, offering direct views of the Nanga Parbat peak.

Beyal Camp

A quiet and peaceful campsite a few hours ahead of Fairy Meadows, closer to the mountain base.

Nanga Parbat Base Camp

The ultimate trekking destination for adventure seekers, offering a close-up view of the majestic glacier.

Adventures in Fairy Meadows

Thrilling Jeep Ride

Experience the world's most dangerous and thrilling jeep track from Raikot Bridge.

Alpine Trekking

Hike through beautiful pine forests and rocky trails alongside glaciers.

Camping & Bonfire

Stay in wooden cottages or tents and enjoy bonfires in freezing temperatures.

Astrophotography

Witness the clearest Milky Way views over the snow-capped mountain peaks.
